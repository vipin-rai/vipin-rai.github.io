E4E - Fabs At A Glance

At E4 Engineer Pvt. Ltd., PESO-approved fuel transport tanks are central to our business. We specialize in designing and fabricating transport and storage tanks of various capacities, tailored precisely to the specifications of filling depots. In addition, E4 Engineer excels in steel fabrication, offering a comprehensive range of services for heavy and large structures, including boiler house structures, ducts, chutes, hoppers, bins, tanks, conveyor systems, venting pipes, reducers, tees, and dampers. Our expertise spans key industries, including cement plants, petrochemicals, power, as well as infrastructure projects like airports, flyovers, and bridges.

Our extensive experience in structural and plate work fabrication includes working with materials such as plain carbon steel, stainless steel, and aluminum. Our highly skilled team of structural steel and piping engineers, fabricators, and welders are adept at handling materials like mild steel, alloy steels, stainless steel, aluminum, and copper. At E4 Engineer Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to delivering products that meet the rigorous quality standards of our clients, supporting the oil and gas, power, and core sector industries with precision and reliability.
